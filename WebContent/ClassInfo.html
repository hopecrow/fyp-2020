Lecturer Info

Dr. Conor Brennan

Dr. Conor Brennan is an Associate Professor in the School of Electronic Engineering, Dublin City University. He teaches a variety of engineering maths course. His research interests are in computational electromagnetics, specifically as applied to EM wave propagation modelling for wireless systems.
